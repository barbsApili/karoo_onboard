Getting Karoo-ted!

Just like the actual “Karoo Karoo” is home to a diverse range of plant and animal species, many of which are uniquely adapted to flourish in its arid conditions, this section houses contents that will aim to allow you to adapt to this new environment and flourish in your own special way (Side note: Our humor is arid but you can drink it.).

Context is key. A set of instructions can be followed blindly and while it might yield the same results as one who has context of what they are doing it won’t allow you to grow. We’ve tried to Karoo-splane everything out - let us know if we are successful at doing this when you’re working through this section.

It’s all well and good to tell you what to do but since no software is complete without a test we’ve included checkpoint “tests” to verify that what you think you’ve done is actually successful. In the event that you go down a path of intense “Stargazing and Astronomy” (Our version of a rabbit hole) once you gain consciousness go to your nearest watering hole and ask any Karoo Team member for help. We really like helping each other out. We really do!

The Karoo has many rock formations and caves and no one person has seen them all so don’t be alarmed if you come across some technology/ topic that you don’t recognise. The main caves we’ll visit will be Python, Docker, Kubernetes and Tango in order to get you to see the big picture of what we do. In time things will become more familiar and we look forward to you excavating areas we haven’t traversed yet.
