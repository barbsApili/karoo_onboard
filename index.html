Getting Karoo-ted!

Just like the actual “Karoo Karoo” is home to a diverse range of plant and animal species, many of which are uniquely adapted to flourish in its arid conditions, this section houses contents that will aim to allow you to adapt to this new environment and flourish in your own special way (Side note: Our humor is arid but you can drink it.).

Context is key. A set of instructions can be followed blindly and while it might yield the same results as one who has context of what they are doing it won’t allow you to grow. We’ve tried to Karoo-splane everything out - let us know if we are successful at doing this when you’re working through this section.

It’s all well and good to tell you what to do but since no software is complete without a test we’ve included checkpoint “tests” to verify that what you think you’ve done is actually successful. In the event that you go down a path of intense “Stargazing” (Our version of a rabbit hole) once you gain consciousness go to your nearest watering hole and ask any Karoo Team member for help. We really like helping each other out. We really do!

The Karoo has many rock formations and caves and no one person has seen them all so don’t be alarmed if you come across some technology/ topic that you don’t recognise. The main caves we’ll visit will be Python, Docker, Kubernetes and Tango in order to get you to see the big picture of what we do. In time things will become more familiar and we look forward to you excavating areas we haven’t traversed yet.

Our Karoo-tine

Life in the Karoo follows daily rhythms dictated by the rising and setting of the sun and so do we as Team Karoo. This section will cover our winning routine that aims to keep us in the famous words of Mufasa in a “delicate balance”. The items listed below are some of the topics we’ll discuss:

How often do we gather and where?

How do we approach our work?

When do we talk to other teams?

When do we present our work?

It might sound like a mouthful but it’ll become second nature to you in time.

Karoo-llection of Repos

What is the Karoo without its interesting features?...empty. We are responsible for a few items that characterize our team’s effort in this huge project. Right now we’re juggling 4 repositories and our aim is to make them work as smooth as a river pebble. We’ll show you how everything is connected. You might get a slight fright but breathe. These repositories were put together by a whole host of awesome people and to understand everything at one go should not be the main aim. We’ll highlight some important parts and also show you how to run them all. As always, when in doubt return to the nearest watering hole and ask any Karoo team mate to break whatever you need to be broken down some more. When we teach we understand more.

Karoo-mmon Processes

Meerkats, like many animals, have daily routines and behaviors that help them survive and thrive in their environment such as foraging, sunbathing etc. We too have things we do repetitively. This section will cover the common tasks you should be aware of so you don’t feel lost when we sometimes do them without even thinking about them deeply. These activities are essential for us to maintain our health, social structure, and survival in the wild.

Karoo-nology

Meerkats are highly social animals that rely on complex communication to maintain their group cohesion and safety. We have our own form of “complex communication” and it may sound like the wind in the Karoo if you aren’t familiar with it. There are so many terms and we’ve tried to capture as many as we can remember but if you hear us dropping acronyms and words that you can’t pick up, please let us know and also help us grow our list.

Karoo-nivorous Learning

Plants in the Karoo have evolved to survive in the region's arid and semi-arid conditions. In essence they’ve had to “learn” what it takes to thrive in the environment. We learn and encourage learning all the time. Without it you’ll cease to improve and limit your potential. There are certain technological and soft skills that we have to constantly learn and master so we’ve highlighted the main areas and as always if we’ve missed something important, you’re one of us now, add it in!
